  • Body Parts
  • Chest: The Chest muscles are used mainly for pushing things.
  • Back: The Back muscles are used mainly for pulling things.
  • Shoulders: The Shoulder muscles are used for many different things, they are incorporated in most movements done. These are important for overall growth of the body.
  • Arms: Consisting mainly of your biceps and triceps, these also compliment all your other upper body parts.
  • Quadraceps: Main muscles of the front part of your leg.
  • Hamstrings: Main muscles of the back part of your leg.
  • Cores: Your core muscles take on the brunt of stabalizing duty. These are extremely important.
